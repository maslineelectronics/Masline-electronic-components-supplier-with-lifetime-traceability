## Electronic Components Supplier with Lifetime Traceability

Lifetime traceability means that each component’s history - from manufacturer production to final delivery is permanently recorded and securely maintained. This record includes manufacturing data, inspection reports, environmental conditions, and supplier documentation, forming an unbroken chain of verification.

### Key Elements of Lifetime Traceability
- **Manufacturer Origin:** Details such as lot codes, batch numbers, and plant identification.  
- **Handling and Storage Data:** Environmental control and material tracking during logistics.  
- **Testing Documentation:** Records of visual, electrical, and functional inspections.  
- **Compliance Certificates:** RoHS, REACH, and ISO documentation included.  
- **Permanent Recordkeeping:** Long-term storage for audit, compliance, and warranty support.  

This ensures customers can confidently trace each component back to its verified and authentic origin.
